about

I'm Ng Ying Xuan, and I'm building a life around computing, teamwork, and steady learning.

My path through school has moved from Jurong Primary School to the School of Science and Technology, Singapore, and then to Anderson Serangoon Junior College, where computing, mathematics, and problem-solving became the subjects I kept returning to.

I enjoy collaborative environments where everyone is trying to make something work a little better than before. That has shaped the way I approach projects, competitions, and volunteer work: with a willingness to help, adapt, and keep learning when the problem changes.

Outside of computing, badminton and piano have stayed with me for a long time. So has community work, whether that meant volunteering with Lions Befrienders and St Luke's Eldercare, facilitating a Python 101 workshop, or helping out in local community programmes.
